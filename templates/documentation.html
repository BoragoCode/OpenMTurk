OpenMTurk Customizable image-labelling tool


The OpenTurk labelling tool was born out of the need to label images in an efficient manner while keeping the data private. The image labelling tool is part of a project to digitalize written or typed documents. The goal is that eventually the programme will be able to recognize not just text, but drawings, diagrams, tables, separation structures, mathematical formulas and symbols, in any kind of written or typed text.
